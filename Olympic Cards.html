Spring 2024 . TYPE II . LISA ROSOWSKY

PARIS 2024 OLYMPIC CARDS

5 X 7 in Xerox Laser Printed on Cardstock. Designed on Adobe Illustrator by Janet Tan Typeface used: Paris2024-Variable, Baskerville

Project Objective: Design a set of 5x7 in informational (and collectible!) cards for athletes from 10 different Olympic events for the 2024 Summer Olympic Games in Paris, then design a simple box package to hold them as a set. distinct.
