FALL 2023 . MY DAILY_ . ROSEMARY AND FISH

MY DAILY_

Xerox Laser Printed and RISO Printed, Pamphlet stiched. Hand Illustrated and layout by Janet Tan. Typeface by Janet Tan.

Project Objective: identify a daily situation that takes up your head space/time and affects your life. Its something you do, think about, observe in others, or in the world that triggers a feeling. This thing absolutely makes you feel a certain way: happy, sad, amazed, angry, annoyed, hopeful, worried, or awestruck. Its persistent and you have something to say about it!
